# 📚 Study Planner

A modern, all-in-one **study management dashboard** designed to help you stay consistent, productive, and fully in control of your workflow.

It combines task management, scheduling, analytics, a focus timer, customization, and an intelligent assistant — all in one clean interface.

## 🧠 Core Features

### 📊 Dashboard
- Track study streaks, study time, and completion rate
- View daily goals and weekly performance
- Quick access to tasks and timer

---

### 📅 Tasks
- Create and manage study tasks
- Filter by priority
- Simple and distraction-free layout

---

### 📚 Subjects
- Organize study areas
- Structure tasks and sessions

---

### 🗓️ Schedule
- Plan weekly sessions
- Multiple session types (regular, Pomodoro, exam prep)
- Visual calendar layout

---

### ⏱️ Focus Timer
- Pomodoro (25 min)
- Short and long breaks
- Deep focus mode
- Runs across the entire app

---

### 📈 Progress Tracking
- Daily goal tracking
- Weekly analytics
- Study heatmap
- Streak system

---

### 🔐 Password Vault

- Clean UI for quick access

- 46+ themes

## 🤖 Study AI (Smart Assistant)

Study Planner includes **Study AI**, a built-in assistant that turns the app into a smart productivity system.

### What it does:
- Helps you plan study sessions
- Suggests focus techniques
- Improves productivity
- Customizes your experience

### Example prompts:
- `Plan a 2-hour session`
- `Give me focus tips`
- `Set my goal to 90 minutes`
- `Make the app more cozy`

### Why it matters:
Instead of just using a planner, you **interact with it**.

Study AI acts like:
- A study coach  
- A productivity assistant  
- A system optimizer
